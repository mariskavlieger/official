About Me

I am a designer, artists and programmer. My work always tells a story about humanity's relationship with the world around them. The interaction between humans and the world is one of the most fascinating things. These days people in the first worlds seem to loose the touch with the earth more and more. We almost forget to look around and apreciate the little stuff. As a child I wasn't capable of throwing away a paper snipped. Because of the meaning I gave it. That's exactly what I try to do with my work. Not only making people aware about their surroundings, but at the same time making them fall in love with the little things and their everyday surroundings. All of this I try to adieve by combining design, art and programming to make the best visualisation of the story of our world.
