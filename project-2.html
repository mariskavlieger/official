Breath of Space

2023 - graduation project

This is a space you don't experience every day. This is because it is alive. The space is breathing. The oxygen of this room is the sunlicht. At night it uses the extra energy it saved from the day. This is why it can breath when there is no sunligt around. This space asks us questions about our relationship with the space around us.

Are we parasites that use the space without thinking about what the best interests of the space itself is? Or would the space be happy with us and does it like interacting and talking to us people? Whatever the relationship is, we have to keep the space on our good side, becuase it's always around us. Maybe we should make sure that the embrace doesn't become a choke hold.
