3D Labyrinth Game

2022 - Arnhem

3D Labyrinth Game is the second collaboration project with Stan de Haas and Yaël Boersma. It is game that combines the physical world with the virual world.

The goal of this game is to lead the marble into through the labirinth to the end point. The game got multiple modes. Players can even create their own labirints. This game can be played with 1 or 2 players. The labirinth which is projected on the ground can be moved around by the players by standing on the Wii balance boards.
