About Me

I am a dutch designer, (light) artist and programmer. My work tells a story about humanity's relationship with the world around them. It asks a certain interaction and reaction from the people. As a child I wasn't capable of throwing away a paper snipped, because I created a big connection with it. That's exactly what I try to do with my work. Not only making people aware about their surroundings, but at the same time making them fall in love with the everyday little things. By combining technology and art, I can make the best representations of our world.
