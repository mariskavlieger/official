Me, My Shadow and I

2022 - Amsterdam

Me, My Shadow and I is a tragic interactive story about the viewer and their shadow. The viewer gets disconnected with their shadow, because the shadow wants to lead a life of it's own. But what is the owner without the shadow once it dissapears? The shadow is their own soul. Their minimalistic self. the real them. If they lost his shadow they looses themself.

In this world we have to learn from a young age who we are and how it is the best way to show this to others. But what if ur body and ur soul want to go diffrent ways? do we even have to figure out who we are, or are we already the person who we are from the beginnning and do we only have to accept that.
